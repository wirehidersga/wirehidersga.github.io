ABOUT

Here at Home Media Solutions, we can help with any cable installation and most electronic devices as well as make recommendations so that you don't have to worry about the cable management that comes with a quality entertainment system.

The company was founded by Chris Roberson, who specializes in home media management - improving existing entertainment systems, designing home theaters and surround sound setups, and most importantnly hiding those ugly wires!

He has managed cable installations for several large internet service providers for 8 years and has private experience managing low voltage wiring.

Most importantly, he is passionate about helping the clients of Home Media Solutions create the domain they envisioned.
